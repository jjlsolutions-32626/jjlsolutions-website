Marketing Services for Small Businesses

Get your business seen by the right people — locally and online — without the big-agency price tag.

Meet Jennie — Jennie leads our marketing services, bringing a practical, results-focused approach to helping local businesses get noticed. Whether you need help with social media, want to show up better in local search, or need someone to handle the marketing tasks you never have time for — she's your point of contact.

Most small businesses in Levy County know they should be "doing something" with marketing, but don't have the time, the knowledge, or the budget for a full-time marketing person. That's where we come in.

We offer practical marketing services sized for small businesses — not enterprise-level campaigns with enterprise-level invoices.

Social Media Management

Staying active on social media takes consistent effort. We handle the parts most business owners struggle with:

  • Regular posting on Facebook, Instagram, or other platforms relevant to your business
  • Content planning so your feed isn't random or abandoned
  • Responding to comments and messages
  • Building engagement with your local community

You stay focused on running your business while your social presence stays active and professional.

Content Creation

Good content helps people find you and trust you. We create:

  • Social media posts and graphics
  • Blog posts and articles
  • Email newsletters
  • Website copy updates
  • Promotional materials

Everything written in your voice, for your audience, with your goals in mind.

Google Business Profile Optimization

When someone searches for your type of business in Levy County, your Google Business Profile is often the first thing they see. We make sure it's working for you:

  • Complete, accurate business information
  • Regular posts and updates
  • Photo optimization
  • Review management and response strategy
  • Q&A monitoring

A well-maintained Google Business Profile can be the difference between getting the call and being invisible.

Local SEO

Showing up in local search results takes more than just having a website. We help with:

  • Local keyword optimization
  • Citation building (getting your business listed in the right directories)
  • Review generation strategies
  • Local content that targets your service area
  • Competitor analysis — understanding who's ranking and why

The goal is simple: when someone in Chiefland, Bronson, Williston, or Cedar Key searches for what you offer, you show up.

Email Marketing

Email is still one of the most effective ways to stay in touch with customers. We set up and manage:

  • Email list building
  • Newsletter design and sending
  • Promotional campaigns
  • Automated sequences (welcome emails, follow-ups)
  • Performance tracking

Brand Design & Graphics

Need your business to look more professional and consistent? We help with:

  • Logo design and refinement
  • Brand color and font guidelines
  • Social media graphics and templates
  • Print materials (business cards, flyers, signage)
  • Presentation design

A cohesive visual identity makes your business look established and trustworthy — even if you're just getting started.

How It Works

We typically work in one of two ways:

Ongoing management — Monthly retainer for consistent social media, content, and marketing support. You get a set number of posts, updates, and hours each month.

Project-based — One-time projects like Google Business Profile setup, brand design, or a specific campaign.

Either way, you get clear deliverables, transparent pricing, and someone who actually understands small business marketing — not a one-size-fits-all package designed for companies ten times your size.

Not Sure Where to Start? Most small businesses benefit most from getting their Google Business Profile optimized first — it's often the quickest win for local visibility. From there, we can build out based on what makes sense for your business and budget.
