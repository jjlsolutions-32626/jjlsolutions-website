Service

Website Design & Maintenance for Small Businesses

Clean, fast, professional websites built to actually work for your business — not just look pretty in a portfolio.

Most small businesses in Levy County either don't have a website, have one that's embarrassingly outdated, or are paying monthly fees for a site they can't update themselves. None of those are good options.

We build straightforward, professional websites that load fast, work on phones, and actually help your business get found. No bloated page builders, no $200/month subscriptions, no mystery fees.

What We Build

We specialize in small business websites that do what they need to do:

  • Business presence sites — Professional homepage, services, about, contact. Everything a potential customer needs to find you and understand what you do.
  • Service business sites — For contractors, consultants, law offices, medical practices, and other service providers who need to establish credibility and generate inquiries.
  • Local business sites — Optimized for local search so people in Levy County actually find you when they're looking for what you offer.

We don't build e-commerce stores or complex web applications — we focus on doing the core small business website really well.

How We're Different

You own your site. We hand over the files, the hosting account, everything. You're not locked into a monthly contract where the site disappears if you stop paying us.

Fast and simple. We build with clean, modern code that loads quickly even on rural internet connections. No 47 plugins that break every time something updates.

Built for search. Proper page titles, meta descriptions, schema markup, mobile optimization, fast load times — the fundamentals that actually help you show up in Google.

Easy to update. We can set you up to make simple text and photo changes yourself, or handle updates for you as part of ongoing maintenance.

Ongoing Maintenance

Websites need occasional attention. Security updates, content changes, fixing things that break, renewing domains and SSL certificates. We offer maintenance plans that handle all of it so you don't have to think about it.

  • Security and software updates
  • Content updates (text changes, new photos, adding pages)
  • Domain and SSL certificate management
  • Uptime monitoring — we find out if your site goes down before your customers do
  • Backup and recovery

Hosting Assistance

Already have a website but struggling with hosting? We can help you:

  • Move to better, faster hosting
  • Set up proper backups
  • Configure email that actually works with your domain
  • Fix SSL certificate issues (the "Not Secure" warning)
  • Troubleshoot why your site is slow or broken

What This Costs

New websites are priced based on scope — a simple 5-page business site costs less than a 20-page service catalog with a blog. We'll give you a clear quote after understanding what you need.

Maintenance plans are monthly and scale with how much attention your site needs. Most small business sites fall into the "check on it, keep it updated, make occasional changes" category.

Already Have a Site That's Not Working? If your current website is slow, outdated, not mobile-friendly, or just embarrassing — sometimes a rebuild is the right call. Sometimes a cleanup is enough. We'll tell you which one honestly.
