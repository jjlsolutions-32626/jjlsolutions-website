Common Questions

Do I need a website if I already have a Facebook page?

Your Facebook page is rented space — Facebook controls it, can change the algorithm, or suspend it without warning. A website is owned space. You control it, no one can take it away, and it ranks in Google searches where Facebook pages don't. You should have both, but a website is your foundation.

What's included in marketing services?

We offer packages or à la carte services. A typical package might include weekly social media posts, monthly Google Business Profile updates, quarterly content creation (blog posts or videos), and ongoing review management. We tailor it to what you actually need, not a one-size-fits-all subscription.

Can you take over an existing website?

Yes. We can take over maintenance, updates, and hosting for sites built on WordPress, static HTML, or most other platforms. If your current provider disappeared or you're paying too much for too little, we can help.

Do you write the content or do I have to?

We can do either. You know your business best, so we prefer collaboration: you give us the information, we write it in a way that works for the web. But if you don't have time, we can interview you and write everything ourselves.
